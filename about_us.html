About Us

For over 53 years, the Monterey Scottish Games and Celtic Association has supported our mission of bringing our community together to experience the rich and multi-faceted Celtic culture, values and history.

The Association is a group of community leaders strengthening the social fabric of our community through educational events, philanthropic activities and the growth of the Monterey Scottish Games and Celtic Festival.

Supported Organizations and Causes
The Association supports and partners with a number of local educational and military organizations including: 4H, NJROTC, military veterans, local students, local schools.

Join and Support Us
Volunteer - Join us in growing the impact of our association and supporting our community all while have fun as a volunteer. We have a wide range of volunteer opportunities for all interests. To learn more, contact us at MontereyCelticFest@gmail.com

Intern program - Apply for our intern program. Have fun supporting an impactful, non-profit organization (501 c3) while enhancing your resume with valuable career experience. Our interns are important, strategic contributors to our most important initiatives. All intern roles receive a monthly stipend. To apply or learn more, contact us at MontereyCelticFest@gmail.com

Donate - As a non-profit 501(C)(3), donations are critical to the continuation and success of our Association. No amount is too small to support our association.
Sponsors
